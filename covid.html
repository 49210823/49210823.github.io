COVID-19 POLICIES AND PROTOCOLS

While we await further direction from the Commonwealth and Mass Youth Soccer relative to indoor youth sports, we are applying the policies and protocols in place for the 2021 season. If and when further direction is available we will update accordingly!

The Directors of the Essex County Youth Futsal League are grateful for the opportunity to provide programming for futsal players, coaches, and referees in our community during the trying times we are all enduring. We appreciate the considerable advice and cooperation of the Pingree School and Massachusetts Youth Soccer in preparing a safe and executable plan to enable our players to play!

We are most appreciative of those of you who choose to participate in this most unusual of seasons, and for your anticipated cooperation with the policies and protocols listed below. We also respect those who opt not to participate this year, as that decision is ultimately a personal one for you and your family.

For the safety of our players, coaches, referees, and staff, and in accordance with requirements placed on us by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, the Massachusetts Youth Soccer Association and the Pingree School, the following protocols will be in place for the 2021-2022 season or until further notice:

  • STAY HOME if not feeling well or have experienced a known exposure risk!!!
  • Masks/face coverings will be required to be properly worn at ALL TIMES while in the building except when hydrating if properly distanced. PLAYER MASKS MUST BE “EAR-LOOP” type, NO GAITERS or AROUND THE HEAD/NECK TIE-BACK STYLES.
  • Referees shall wear masks and exclusively use electronic/hand operated whistles.
